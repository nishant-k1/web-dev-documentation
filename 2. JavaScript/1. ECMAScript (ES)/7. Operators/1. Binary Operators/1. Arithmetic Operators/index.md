# Arithmetic Operators

- `**` (Exponentiation)

- `*` (Multiplication)
- `/` (Division)
- `%` (Modulus)

- `+` (Addition)
- `-` (Subtraction)

- `++` (Increment)
- `--` (Decrement)

## precedence

Exponentiation
Multiplication
Division
Remainder
Addition
Subtraction

`()` – Overrides precedence (highest).

`**` – Evaluates right-to-left (e.g., `2 ** 3 ** 2 = 2 ** (3 ** 2) = 2 ** 9 = 512`).

`*,  /,  %` – Same precedence, evaluated left-to-right.

`+,  -` – Same precedence, evaluated left-to-right (lowest).
