# Render – Stable Props & Inline Functions

Unstable props and new inline functions on every render can cause **unnecessary re-renders**.

---

## Problems

- Creating new objects/arrays/functions inside JSX:

```jsx
<List items={[1, 2, 3]} />
<Button onClick={() => doSomething(id)} />
```

- Memoized children (`React.memo`) re-render because props **references** keep changing.

---

## Solutions

- Hoist static objects/arrays out of the component or memoize them with `useMemo`.
- Memoize callbacks with `useCallback`.

---

## Interview Line

> “I keep props stable by avoiding inline object/function creation in JSX and by memoizing callbacks when passing them to memoized children.”
