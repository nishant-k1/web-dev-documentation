# Daily Schedule (6–8 Hours of Focused Preparation)

---

## **Morning: Core Concepts (2–3 hours)**

### **8:00 AM – 9:00 AM: JavaScript / TypeScript Concepts**

- Review ES6+ features, algorithms, and TypeScript topics
- Solve **1–2 easy coding problems** related to the concepts learned

### **9:00 AM – 9:15 AM: Short Break**

- Stretch, grab a coffee, or meditate briefly

### **9:15 AM – 10:15 AM: React / Next.js Deep Dive**

- Work on React concepts, reusable components, hooks
- Explore a Next.js feature (SSR, SSG, routing, API routes, layouts, etc.)

### **10:15 AM – 10:45 AM: Reflect & Take Notes**

- Summarize your learning in your own words
- Create/update your personal cheatsheets

---

## **Midday: Hands-On Practice (3–4 hours)**

### **11:00 AM – 12:30 PM: Coding Challenges**

- Solve **2–3 problems** on LeetCode / HackerRank
- Focus on **medium difficulty** (arrays, strings, trees, DP)

### **12:30 PM – 1:30 PM: Lunch & Rest**

- Eat and relax to recharge

### **1:30 PM – 3:00 PM: Project Work**

- Work on a mini project (React + Node.js) or refactor an older one
- Implement features like **authentication, API integration, testing**, etc.

---

## **Afternoon / Evening: Interview Prep (2–3 hours)**

### **3:30 PM – 4:30 PM: System Design / Behavioral Questions**

- Learn a system design topic (caching, scaling, CDNs, DBs)
- Prepare for HR questions using the **STAR method**

### **4:30 PM – 4:45 PM: Short Break**

- Quick walk or music

### **4:45 PM – 5:45 PM: Mock Interviews or Whiteboard Practice**

- Practice coding with a timer
- Explain your solution aloud
- Sketch system designs or explain past project architecture

---

## **Night: Light Study & Networking (Optional)**

### **7:00 PM – 8:00 PM: Review Notes & Network**

- Revise your notes from the day
- Apply for jobs, reach out to connections on LinkedIn

### **8:00 PM – 9:00 PM: Relaxation**

- Unwind with music, a book, or a hobby

---

# **Tips for Staying Productive**

- **Pomodoro Technique:** 25 min focused work + 5 min break
- **Switch Focus Areas:** Avoid studying one topic the entire day
- **Health Matters:** Hydrate, eat well, sleep 7–8 hours
